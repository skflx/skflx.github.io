Emergent Technology Projects

I'm passionate about developing new technologies to address challenges in hearing healthcare and surgical practice.

Speech-in-Noise Processing Technology

2022 - Present Hearing Technology

Working on improving speech recognition in challenging listening environments by enhancing both software and hardware solutions in hearing devices. This technology aims to address one of the most significant challenges for individuals with hearing loss.

The project focuses on finding new approaches to better isolate speech from background noise, particularly in complex acoustic environments with multiple speakers or competing sounds.

Surgical Livestreaming Platform

Designed and implemented a technical system for livestreaming surgical procedures to medical students, including audio/video setup and sterile exoscope mounting for open procedures.

Clinical Projects

My clinical work focuses on improving patient care through innovative approaches to surgical education, treatment protocols, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

2022 - Present

Surgery Livestreaming Elective

Designed and organized curriculum for pre-clerkship medical students to gain exposure to surgical specialties through live-streaming procedures. Responsible for identifying appropriate surgical cases, audio/video setup, mounting sterile video exoscope in open procedures, and facilitating communication between live-streaming and operative teams.

2020 - Present

HAVEN Free Clinic

Provided primary care to uninsured and underinsured patients as a Senior Clinical Team Member. Presented assessments and plans to attendings, placed orders, followed-up with patients regarding medical concerns, specialist referrals, and ancillary needs. Supervised and mentored junior clinical team members through their history-taking and physical exam skills.

2021 - Present

Point of Care Ultrasound Teaching

Provided hands-on training in essential ultrasound skills to medical students through weekly small-group lessons designed to align with the MS1 curriculum, enhancing diagnostic capabilities for future clinicians.
