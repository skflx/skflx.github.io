Hi, I'm @skflx.MD

Resident Otolaryngologist, Researcher and Innovator, Humanist

A surgeon-in-training on our speck of dust suspended in a remote sunbeam, lover of nature and the outdoors, science and technology enthusiast, and humanist. About Me

I'm a resident otolaryngologist in the Pacific Northwest, with a special interest in cognitive frameworks and emergent technologies, especially as they relate to surgical education. With a background in neuroscience and as a person with hearing loss, I work to bring new perspectives to medical and surgical challenges - on individual, institutional, and systemic levels.

Beyond medicine, I'm an artist, musician, and outdoors enthusiast with way too many hobbies.
